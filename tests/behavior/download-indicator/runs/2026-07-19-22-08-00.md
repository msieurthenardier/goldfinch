- **Completion state — PASS.** Navigating a new tab to

  became visible and read `Downloads — 1 recently completed`.
- **Popup — PASS.** The per-window sheet exposed a labeled `role="dialog"` with one completed
  `download-fixture.bin` row, filename and folder buttons, and an `Open downloads page` footer.
- **Capacity mechanics — PASS.** The live popup computed a finite `max-height`; its dedicated
  `.dl-list` reported `overflow-y: auto`, leaving the footer outside the scrolling row region.
- **Footer action — PASS.** Activating the footer closed the sheet and opened an active
  `goldfinch://downloads` internal tab.
- **App-scoped new-window hydration — PASS.** A second real window booted after completion and its
  chrome immediately showed `Downloads — 1 recently completed` without another download event.
- **Accessibility — PASS.** `npm run a11y -- --tags=wcag2a,wcag2aa,wcag21a,wcag21aa
  --url=http://127.0.0.1:8000/a11y-media/` reported no new violations, including
  `downloads-button` and `sheet:downloads`.

## Post-review amendment recheck

After the adversarial review fixes, the live app was restarted and the gate was repeated. Persisted
history intentionally contained one expired prior epoch plus one current-epoch completion. The first
window hydrated **one**, not both; a new real download raised it to **two**; the popup rendered exactly
those two current-epoch rows; and a newly created second window also hydrated exactly two. This is the
live discriminator for the gap-defined epoch policy—an expired epoch was not revived.

The capped audit popup was also forced to a 320 px short-window height: 25 rows produced
`scrollHeight 725 > clientHeight 270`, the outer dialog stayed exactly 320 px tall, and Tab from the
fixed footer reached the labeled, `tabIndex=0` scroll region. The WCAG 2.1 A/AA sweep remained green in
that actual-overflow state. Two additional deterministic fixture downloads from the amendment runs were
hash-checked and moved recoverably to Trash as `goldfinch-pr107-download-fixture-r2-20260719.bin` and
`goldfinch-pr107-download-fixture-r3-20260719.bin`.
