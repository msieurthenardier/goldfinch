survival claim itself required a re-run.

## Disposition

Three spec errata folded by the FD before run 2:
1. Step 2 gains a **commit-settle gate** (poll page-2 commit before
   navigating; then poll history length === 2 — load-bearing for steps 6/8).
2. Step 3's boot bracket polls until the boot census settles (an immediate
   read can return `[]` pre-dom-ready; `getChromeTarget` is transiently
   `url:""`).
3. Preconditions gain the foreground-first read-order note (readAxTree on a
   background tab activates it).
