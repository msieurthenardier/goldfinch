### Validator closing
8/8 PASS at the user-perceivable level. Two cross-cutting **pre-existing** a11y follow-ups (NOT regressions from this flight — the container menu predates it and was preserved as-is per DD1): (1) ARIA contract mismatch — `#new-tab-menu` advertises `aria-haspopup="menu"` but the popup is plain `<button>`s with no `role=menu`/`menuitem`/arrow roving; (2) keyboard reachability — Tab from inside a `<webview>` is trapped, so the pill isn't reachable from the page top via plain Tab. Minor: menu-item activation drops focus to `<body>` while Escape restores it to the trigger. These belong in future a11y / `tab-keyboard-operability` follow-ups.
